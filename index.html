    Why Nyla Foodies

    Cooked the way home taught us

    Every dish leaves our kitchen the way it would leave a grandmother's pot — slow-cooked, generously seasoned, and served without shortcuts.

    🌽 Traditional Recipes

    Sadza, mopane worms, muriwo unedovi and other dishes rooted in Zimbabwean home cooking, made the way our own families make them.

    Our Story

    Preserving Zimbabwe's culinary heritage

    Established in July 2026 on the corner of First Street and Jason Moyo Avenue, Nyla Foodies was built to give traditional Zimbabwean cooking a proper table in the heart of Harare — for locals who grew up on this food, and for visitors discovering it for the first time.

    We cook with fresh, locally sourced ingredients in a warm, family-friendly space, staying true to the recipes and techniques passed down through Zimbabwean households.
